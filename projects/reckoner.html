The Reckoner

I was involved with The Reckoner from 2013 - 2016, Marc Garneau CI's high school newspaper. I was the News Manager for the independent newspaper that aimed to serve as a voice for the student population. We held the student council and school administration accountable for their actions, called for policy changes, united the student body by sharing information, and fostered a creative and engaging team environment. We were voted Ontarioâ€™s Best High School Newspaper by the Toronto Star in 2016.

We had print editions every month or two, and developed new segments and columns. My personal favourites were the Teacher Features and the infographics. In the Teacher Features, we spoke to our educators about their lives, teaching philosophies, and miscellaneous topics. We regularly produced infographics on political elections in Canada and summarized platforms and policies within in our own student council elections.
