Undercover Kindness

I started this with a friend in Grade 10, and have had the pleasure of seeing it continue to develop into McGill University. Undercover Kindness is an initiative that has a simple goal: we want to encourage more Random Acts of Kindness on campus and in the local community. We host “pass-it-on” initiatives, sparking kind acts. We wrote letters thanking bus drivers for their hard work. If we see more smiles around, we’ll be happy.
